The purpose of this website is to assess whether you are an auditory, visual, or kinesthetic learner and provide resources tailored to your type of learning.

Most learners can be categorized into one of three broad groups: Visual, Auditory, and Kinesthetic learners. Individuals that fall within different groups often find that certain study techniques, teaching practices, and more differ in effectiveness. We look to redesign learning, minimize study time, and optimize academic, professional, and creative output by enabling effective identification of learning styles, paving the path to the next level of learning.



Pathways is a collaborative effort for students, by students. Originally developed at MIT, our intuitive methodology is designed to foster meaningful discussion, connecting student experience to student learning styles.
